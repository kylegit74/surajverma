Suraj Verma | Professional Wedding Photographer Birmingham

Your wedding day only comes once. Our wedding photographer Birmingham ensures that every smile is captured forever. Our cameras follow you from getting ready until the last dance. We capture photos of your ceremony, family groups, and all the fun moments in between.

Suraj Verma and his team are well-versed in Birmingham's top wedding venues. From grand hotels to country gardens, we've photographed weddings everywhere. Our job is easy - capture your love story naturally. No awkward standing or stiff poses. Just real moments that capture who you are as a couple.

Wedding photography is all about being prepared for anything. The tears at vows, grandparents laughing, kids being silly, friends celebrating. We catch it all. Our experience tells us where to be when special moments occur. We don't get in your way and you get beautiful photos.

Suraj Verma Wedding Videography Vibes

Experienced Wedding Videographer Birmingham

First, we start with engagement shoots around Birmingham. These will make you feel at ease with our cameras and provide you with beautiful photos to use as save-the-dates or wedding invitations. We also capture pre-wedding events such as hen dos and stag parties in case you would like those memories to be captured as well.

We make cinematic films on your wedding day that narrate your love story. Our videographers record the mood, the voices, and the motion that cannot be captured in photos. We also carry out drone photography of spectacular aerial shots of your venue and guests. Live streaming allows your family in other countries to view your ceremony live.

We create custom photo books and canvas prints to your home after your wedding. We make highlight reels to share on social media, make thank you videos to share with guests, and design digital galleries that you can share with everyone. Some couples book us again for maternity shoots, baby photos, or anniversary celebrations when they want to add to their story.

We also do corporate events, birthday celebrations, family reunions, and christening ceremonies. We are ready to record any special moment in Birmingham that requires professional documentation.
